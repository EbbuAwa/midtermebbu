Locating Buildings and Facilities

Campuses often have numerous buildings for different purposes like classrooms, libraries, administrative offices, dining halls, recreational centers, etc. Navigating helps individuals find these locations efficiently.

Finding Specific Rooms or Offices

Within buildings, there are specific rooms or offices where classes are held, professors hold office hours, or administrative tasks are conducted. Navigation aids in locating these spaces.

Time Management

Efficient navigation helps individuals manage their time better, ensuring they reach their destinations promptly, whether it's for classes, meetings, or other commitments.

Orientation for Newcomers

For new students, faculty, or visitors, navigating the campus helps them familiarize themselves with the layout, making it easier for them to find their way around.

Our Mission

Virtual tours have the potential to break down barriers of physical distance and accessibility, allowing people to explore and engage with places they might never have the opportunity to visit in person.

Our Plan

Our plan for a virtual tour of the campus involves creating an immersive experience using VR technology. Users can navigate through key locations, access information about facilities and programs, and engage with interactive elements.

Our Vision

Our vision for the virtual tour of our campus is to offer an immersive and comprehensive experience that mirrors the real-world campus visit. Utilizing cutting-edge VR technology, users can explore facilities, interact with campus life, attend virtual events, and access detailed information about programs and services.
